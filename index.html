Tile Adhesive

CEYCA-MAX CM-1

Economy grade POLYMER-modified dry set mortar containing Portland cement, silica, polymer & additive for good adhesion. Mixed with water for installation of small and medium size wall & floor tiles in interior area

CEYCA-MAX CM-2

CM-2 is a ‘Select’ Portland cement based thin set mortar for installation of small and medium size tile on various substrates. For fixing large size tile improved performance like additional work ability, higher bonding strength, flexibility and fixing large size tile in exterior application add CS TA SB latex admix.

CEYCA-MAX CM-3

CM-3 is excellent performance polymer modified thin set adhesive with non sag property and high mechanical property. This is highly preferable for fixing large size tiles and natural stone for floor and wall application. You can mix with CS TA SB admix for improved flexibility and high bond strength of tile or stone on special grade plywood. Polymer modified to provide a strong bond on tiles with low porosity.

CEYCA-MAX CM-4

CM-4 versatile tile adhesive is an excellent quality specially prepared polymer modified thin set adhesive for exterior and interior installation of ceramic tile, glass tile, mosaic tile, natural stones etc on difficult substrates. CS-4 versatile thin set adhesive is designed to mix with water, has a convenient open time with high adhesion and work ability

Epoxy Tile Grout

CEYCA-MAX Epoxy 3 part

A three component, fully solid epoxy grout consists of Part A and Part B in Leak proof bottles and Part C in a separate pouch all packed in a plastic pail. It is hygienic, stain-free and chemical resistant grout. It does not absorb water, thus resists bacterial and fungal growth. It is a high abrasion and impact resistant grout for tiles.

CEYCA-MAX Epoxy 2 part

Epoxy tile grout is a type of grouting material used to fill the gaps between tiles in various applications, such as bathrooms, kitchens, and other tiled surfaces. Unlike traditional cement-based grouts, epoxy tile grout consists of two parts that need to be mixed together before application. These two parts are usually referred to as the epoxy resin and the epoxy hardener.

Waterproofing

CEYCA-MAX SBR LATEX

CEYCA-MAX LATEX SBR is a contracted latex admix is diluted with water on job site and then mixed with Adhscreed Auto cure or CERA PLASTER Readymade PLASTER to make screed, plasters, coatings and thick mortar bends to get highly improved physical property mortar beds to get highly improved physical property & mechanical characteristics and it can be used for interior and exterior application.

CEYCA-MAX Brush & Coat 2K

CM BRUSH COAT (Two component): Flexible Elastomeric Cementitious waterproof coating. Its two component acrylic polymer modified cementitous high performance coating with crack bridging properties for external / Internal waterproofing & water retaining structures, Water tank, Roof, Basement, for forming portative film below tiles in Swimming pool, Shower, Kitchen, bathroom & other wet areas.

CEYCA-MAX Roof coat

CM Roof Coat -1001 is a heavy duty acrylic based elastomeric waterproof coating system used for terraces. It is made from highly elastic & resilient acrylic polymer, fungicides, properly graded fillers weather durable pigment, micro-fibres, additives, etc., It is used as a liquid applied waterproofing membrane for all types of building terraces. Can be used for flat & sloping roofs.
